About the Project

Making government schemes easier to find

Millions of citizens miss out on welfare schemes they are eligible for simply because they don't know these schemes exist, or aren't sure which one applies to them. Government Scheme Predictor uses a short, guided form to match a citizen's personal and socio-economic profile against a database of government schemes, instantly surfacing the most relevant one.

How it works

  1. You fill in basic details: personal information and a few eligibility yes/no answers.
  2. The app cleans and encodes your answers into a feature set.
  3. A trained classification model (or a transparent rule-based matcher when no model is present) scores each scheme.
  4. You receive the best-matching scheme, along with a confidence score and a link to the official page.

Technology

Built with Flask, Bootstrap, and scikit-learn. This is a student / demo project and is not affiliated with any official government body.
